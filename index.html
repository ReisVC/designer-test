A famous fashion designer from west coast of America...

Neon Roots

A collection that blends elements of Amazonian Indigenous fashion with fluorescent tech fabrics. The goal is to explore the connection between tradition and the future.

CyberGaia

Dresses 3D-printed using recyclable materials inspired by nature and futuristic video game aesthetics. Designed for virtual runways and avatars.

Vortex

A conceptual study of garments for use in zero-gravity environments, like space stations. Features magnetic fabrics and seamless construction.

Re-Vero

Garments made entirely from leftover industrial uniforms. The collection aims to dress young people from underserved areas with style and environmental awareness.
